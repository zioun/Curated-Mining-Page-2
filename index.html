FAQ — Institutional Hosting Partnerships

Why not go directly to a hosting company? +

Hosting providers profit by filling racks, not by protecting investor capital. We partner with you to negotiate terms, audit facilities, and hold hosts accountable through SLAs.

How do I know if a hosting facility is reliable? +

Reliability is measured through uptime history, repair queue transparency, energy contract stability, and counterparty solvency. We conduct due diligence using financial records, site inspections, and operational audits to ensure facilities meet institutional standards.

Can I tour facilities before committing? +

Yes. We can arrange private site tours at vetted facilities, allowing you to inspect infrastructure, cooling systems, and security protocols before signing any contracts.

What scale of operations can hosting partners handle? +

Facilities in our network accommodate projects from a few megawatts up to 100MW+ institutional deployments. Containerized solutions are available for more flexible fleet structures.

Are renewable power options available? +

Many vetted hosts operate in regions with abundant renewable energy — hydro, wind, or solar — ensuring both sustainability and cost efficiency.
